Process
How it works
A four-stage cycle designed to be honest, structured, and psychologically safe for every participant.
01
🗂
Cycle Setup
HR or the manager defines the review cycle, selects competencies, and assigns reviewers across all four feedback directions.
02
📝
Feedback Collection
All reviewers complete structured questionnaires covering ratings, behavioural anchors, and open-ended qualitative input. Fully anonymous.
03
🧠
AI Synthesis
The AI engine aggregates scores, detects perception gaps between self and others, and identifies blind spots and hidden strengths automatically.
04
🌱
Development Plan
A personalised development plan is generated, with targeted recommendations tied to specific competency gaps revealed by the multi-rater data.
What We Measure
Core competencies
Six universal dimensions that determine how effectively someone leads, collaborates, and delivers in any organisation or industry.
🧭
Leadership & Vision
Sets clear direction, inspires commitment, and navigates ambiguity with confidence. Measured across all four rater groups.
💬
Communication
Clarity, active listening, constructive feedback delivery, and ability to adapt communication style across audiences.
🤲
Collaboration
Contribution to team goals, peer support, conflict resolution, and cross-functional partnership effectiveness.
⚡
Execution & Delivery
Reliability, quality of output, deadline adherence, and ability to prioritise under pressure. Critical for peer and manager views.
🔄
Adaptability
Openness to change, learning agility, and resilience when strategies shift or new challenges arise unexpectedly.
🌱
Growth Mindset
Proactive learning, receptiveness to feedback, and the ability to turn developmental input into visible behavioural change.
